Dan Meager's Favourite Films

Top 3 Films

In Bruges

One of the funniest films I've seen. The casting is impeccable and the writing is genius.

The Dark Knight

The greatest superhero film ever made. Christopher Nolan knows exactly how to make a blockbuster. And who can forget Heath Ledgers performance as the Joker? The greatest performance as a villain ever? I think so.

The Usual Suspect

I can always remember how amazed I was when the twist began to unravel. My jaw was on the flaw.
